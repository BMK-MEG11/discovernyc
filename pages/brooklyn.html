NBA Brooklyn Nets

Are you a fan of Basketball? If you are then seeing the Brooklyn Nets is a must and even if you are not it doesn’t matter as watching an NBA game in the Barclays Center in Brooklyn is gonna be worth your time and money either way. It's an all-around great experience with the electric atmosphere of people cheering on their favorite team and the show surrounding the game makes it something you shouldn’t miss out on. You can also spend the first half of your day exploring the rest of Brooklyn and ending the day with the game.

Places to visit

DUMBO

DUMBO or Down Under the Manhattan Bridge Overpass is a beautiful neighborhood in Brooklyn with a great view of Manhattan and the rest of the city. It also makes for a great spot for family photos or maybe a new profile picture as I'm sure you’ll see once you get there. From there on you can take a nice walk through some parks along the East River enjoying the view of the city or maybe take a bike to explore Brooklyn. The area also is home to quite a few different art galleries you could visit during your stay. When making your way back to Manhattan you also have the option to take a ferry to get yet another way to look at the city.
