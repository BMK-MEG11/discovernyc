Get around the city

So many ways to get around the city but which one should you use?

Subway

The subway is a great way to get around NYC as its pretty fast as well as relatively cheap compared to taking a cab everywhere.

Bike

Using a bike to make your way through New York is really enjoyable. Unlike using the subway, you get to see a lot of the city while going to your destination.

Cab

While taking a cab can be pretty expensive and not necessarily the fastest depending on traffic its definitely the most comfortable way to get around and see a lot of the city.
